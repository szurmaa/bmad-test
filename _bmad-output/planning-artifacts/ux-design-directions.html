Recommendation

Use Direction 01 as the base, then borrow selective structure from Direction 03.

Direction 01 best matches the product promise: it feels emotionally safe, clear in under a few seconds, and distinct without reading as a game. Direction 03 contributes cleaner information framing for reroll state, offline confidence, and secondary metadata. That combination keeps the app calm while avoiding vagueness.

  • Take the centered ritual composition, warm palette, and gentle emphasis from Direction 01.
  • Take the clearer state chips and task-card framing from Direction 03.
  • Avoid the heavier atmosphere of Direction 05 for the main product surface, but keep its reveal-staging ideas for motion.
  • Avoid making Direction 04 the default because it gets closer to playful-app territory than the product needs.
